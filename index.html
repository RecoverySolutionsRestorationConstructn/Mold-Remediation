Mold Remediation

Once mold has invaded your home, it can be incredibly difficult to remove. Fortunately, the experienced mold removal team at Recovery Solutions specializes in mold remediation.

While it’s a naturally occurring substance, mold can have detrimental effects on your health if it’s not detected early. From a stuffy nose to serious respiratory issues, it’s important to contact a qualified mold removal expert to remedy this problem as soon as possible.

Read Post

Mold removal can be a lengthy and complicated process. In addition to removing obvious affected areas, you need an expert to inspect the rest of your home for signs of mold and create an effective treatment plan for removal.

Our experienced restoration specialists can help you navigate the mold remediation process with minimal headache and maximum results. We will use negative air chambers to isolate contaminated areas and prevent the spread of mold during the cleaning process. Then, using powerful air filters and HEPA vacuums, our team will clear the air within your home or office removing lingering mold spores and prepare it for deep sanitization.

When left untreated, mold emits a strong odor that can stain your furniture and other belongings. After we remove the mold from your home, we will use a variety of advanced fogging techniques to deodorize your property and leave your home or business odor-free.
